WRONG FOR HAY & HAY COLLABORATE WITH PECKHAM REFRESHMENT ROOMS

WRONG FOR HAY & HAY have teamed up with the critically acclaimed Peckham Refreshment Rooms to create a destination pop-up restaurant for the 2013 London Design Festival. Offering drinks together with charcuterie, fine cheeses, rich viennoiserie, the former St. Stephen's Club and terrace overlooking St. James' Park will incorporate both WRONG FOR HAY and HAY designs to make a unique design and gastronomic destination.

34 Queen Anne's Gate, London SW1H 9AB
Bookings for lunch & dinner: reservations@wrongforhay.com
Bar and terrace open for drinks/snacks: 14-22 of September
Sat 14 to Tues 17: 9am - 11pm
Wed 18 to Thu 19: 9am - 5pm
Fri 20 to Sun 22: 9am - 11pm
